
Checkers is played on a standard 64 square  board. 
Only the 32 dark colored squares are used in play. 
Each player  begins the  game  with  12 pieces, or 
checkers, placed in the three rows closest  to him 
or her.The object of the game is to capture all of 
your opponent's checkers or  position  your pieces 
so that your opponent has no available moves.
